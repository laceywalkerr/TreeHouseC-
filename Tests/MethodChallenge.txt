**Quiz Question 1 of 7
What is a method?

Choose the correct answer below:	   
A	
A group of code statements that are put together to perform a particular task.

B	
A template that objects are created from.

C	
A place in memory to store information.

    Answer:
    A	
    A group of code statements that are put together to perform a particular task.
